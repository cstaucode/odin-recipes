Street Corn

What You Need:

  • 12 ears of corn
  • 3/4 cups mexican crema
    • Sour cream works well too
  • 3/4 cups mayo
  • 1 1/2 cups Cotija cheese
    • Feta cheese is a good substitue if you cant find Cotija cheese
  • 6 cloves crushed or minced garlic
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Juice and the zest of 1-2 limes
  • 3/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 1/2 tbs chipolte chile powder
    • Regular chile powder or paprika will taste good as well

Directions:

  1. Heat your over to 400° F
  2. Husk the corn and cover them in tinfoil and into an oven safe tray
  3. Bake the corn for approximately 40 minutes
  4. Mix all of the sauce ingredients: Sour cream, mayo, cheese, garlic, lime juice, lime zest, and cilantro
  5. Lather the corn in the sauce
  6. Add chipolte chile powder to taste
  7. Add extra cheese or cilanto to taste
