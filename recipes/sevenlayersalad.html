Seven Layer Salad

What You Need:

Dressing Ingredients:

  • 2-2 1/2 cups of mayo
  • 2 Tbs. sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • Subsitute with yogurt if desired

Salad Ingredients:

  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 cucumber
    • Sub tomato if desired
  • 1 cup (or more) frozen peas.
  • 1 1/2 cups (2-3 Stalks) celery
  • 1 big red onion
  • 6 oz Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 lbs crispy cooked bacon;crumbled
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs

Directions:

  1. Cook the bacon using your preferred method and set aside
  2. Hard-boil 2 eggs, peel, and set them aside
  3. Layer the lettuce, cucumber/tomato, frozen peas, celery, and onion. Make sure the lettuce is on the bottom and NO mixing
  4. Mix the dressing ingredients together and pour evenly over the salad
  5. Top off with cheese, bacon bits, and your boiled eggs
