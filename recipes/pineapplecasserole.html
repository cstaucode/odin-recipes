Pineapple Casserole

What You Need:

  • 2 20oz cans of pineapple chunks in juice
    • Make sure to set aside 6 Tbs. of juice
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 5 Tbs. flour
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 sleeve Ritz crackers; crushed

Directions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350° F
  2. Stir together the sugar, flour, and pineapple juice
  3. Add the pineapples to the mixture
  4. Put the mixture in an oven safe pan, and top with the shredded cheese
  5. Crush the Ritz crackers in a bowl and mix with melted butter
  6. Spoon the Ritz crumbles atop of the dish
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
